Executive Summary

Thai Heritage
Reimagined

VERTEX Company is dedicated to bringing "Khao Lam" — Thailand's ancient bamboo sticky rice — to the world stage, blending cultural heritage with modern innovation and uncompromising quality.

We believe the finest ingredients, time-honored techniques, and contemporary refinement can transform a beloved local tradition into a globally celebrated luxury experience.

"Represent Thai Sticky Rice in Bamboo to the International Eyes"

Company and Factory based in Chiang Mai — the cultural heart of Northern Thailand

Market 1 · Flagship
Original Collection

Khao Lam Original

Traditional Khao Lam in hand-selected bamboo tubes, slow-cooked with premium glutinous rice, black beans, and fresh coconut milk. Finished with genuine edible gold leaf — an elegant, meaningful gift that reflects the true essence of Northern Thailand.

🍘
Market 2 · Snack
Crispy Series

Khao Lam Crispy Rice

Crispy rice crackers born from authentic Khao Lam — golden on the outside, tender within, with a gentle sweetness from coconut milk. Perfect as a premium snack or a distinctive souvenir in elegant packaging.

🎂
Market 3 · Patisserie
Dessert Line

Khao Lam Cake

A Khao Lam fusion cake uniting the warm fragrance of bamboo rice with patisserie artistry. Silky coconut cream mousse, layers of black glutinous rice, and a crisp golden topping — beauty you can taste.

🍪
Market 4 · Artisan
Confectionery

Khao Lam Macaron

Authentic French macarons reimagined with Thai Khao Lam — a delicate shell that shatters into a lush coconut-bamboo rice filling. Perfectly crafted for the premium market and high-end gifting.
